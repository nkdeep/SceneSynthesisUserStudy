Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully.

This survey intends to test the legibility of the letters from the English alphabet. Legibility is "the quality of being clear enough to read".

The questions you will be presented in this survey are two fold.
  1. When given a single image, identify the letter.
  2. When given a pair of images of the a letter, identify the image containing the more legible form.

You will be presented with 22 questions, which may take at most 7 minutes to complete on average.
